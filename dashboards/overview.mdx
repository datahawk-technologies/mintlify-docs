DataHawk provides ready-to-use dashboard templates on top of your managed database.

## Power BI templates

Available template families include:

- Seller Finances and Supply Chain
- Product
- Search
- Advertising
- Market Intelligence
- Master Vendor
- DSP Ads

These templates are delivered as `.pbix` files so you can customize them for your own reporting needs.

## Seller Analytics dashboard

The Seller Analytics dashboard is built for Seller Central managers and combines:

- traffic
- sales and orders
- profit and margin
- advertising
- inventory

To use the full dashboard, connect:

- a Seller Central account
- an advertising account
- COGS data if you want complete P&L tabs

## Main dashboard areas

Typical sections in the Seller Analytics experience include:

- **Executive report** for KPI monitoring
- **Product unit economics** for ASIN-level profitability
- **Traffic** for page views, sessions, and conversion
- **Sales orders** for operational sales metrics
- **P&L** for revenue, fees, COGS, and profit
- **Advertising** for campaign and product performance
- **Inventory** for stock status and replenishment planning
- **Explorer** for cross-metric analysis
- **Download center** for custom matrix exports

## Dashboard workflow

1. Add a destination.
2. Open the dashboard template.
3. Replace the database credentials with the destination credentials from DataHawk.
4. Refresh the dataset.
5. Filter by marketplace, brand, seller, tag, ASIN, or time period.

## Good fit

Use the dashboard layer when you want packaged reporting with minimal setup. Use the database directly when you need fully custom analytics.
