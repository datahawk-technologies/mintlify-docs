DataHawk structures Amazon and Walmart data so teams can answer high-value business questions without building the pipeline themselves.

## Who it helps

- Sellers use DataHawk to understand revenue drops, Buy Box changes, conversion, margin, and replenishment risk.
- Vendors use it to analyze shipped versus ordered revenue, traffic, forecasting, and purchase order execution.
- Advertising managers use it to compare ACoS, TACoS, ROAS, search terms, campaign efficiency, and DSP outcomes.
- Market and finance teams use it to estimate share, benchmark price bands, analyze returns, and break down fee and profit burden.

## What you get

- Pre-built dashboards in Power BI and Looker Studio.
- Hosted structured data in destinations such as BigQuery and Snowflake.
- Apps such as Alerts, Insights, Sherlock, and AI Copywriter.
