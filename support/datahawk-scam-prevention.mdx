title: "DataHawk Scam Prevention"
---

DataHawk's only official domain is [datahawk.co](https://datahawk.co/). If you receive suspicious communication, contact `contact@datahawk.co`.

## Important reminders

- DataHawk does not recruit through WhatsApp or Telegram.
- DataHawk does not issue employer IDs.
- Similar-looking domains are not affiliated with DataHawk.
