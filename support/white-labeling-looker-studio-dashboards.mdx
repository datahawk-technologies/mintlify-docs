---
title: "White-labeling and Client-Level Data Access for Looker Studio"
---

This guide outlines three delivery models: duplicate dashboards per client, a master dashboard with filtered URLs, or a portal that embeds dashboards behind controlled access.

## Best fit by scale

- Fewer than 10 clients: duplicated dashboards
- Roughly 10 to 50 clients: master dashboard plus filtered URLs
- 50 or more clients: dedicated portal layer
