DataHawk turns Amazon and Walmart data into structured outputs for reporting, monitoring, and decision-making.

## Core capabilities

- Connect seller, vendor, advertising, product, keyword, and category data.
- Model and enrich raw marketplace inputs.
- Deliver curated data through hosted databases and dashboard templates.
- Support workflows with built-in apps such as Alerts, Insights, Sherlock, and AI Copywriter.
