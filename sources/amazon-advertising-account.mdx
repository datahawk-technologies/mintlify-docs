---
title: "Amazon Private Data - Advertising Account"
---

Connect Amazon Advertising to access campaign, keyword, target, and product-level ad performance.

## Covered areas

- Sponsored Products
- Sponsored Brands
- Sponsored Display
- DSP, when available on the connected user

## Notes

This account is separate from Seller Central and Vendor Central. Initial sync timing and finalized data can lag Amazon's own UI.
