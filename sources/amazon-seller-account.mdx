## Your Seller Central account

Amazon Seller Central is the platform sellers use to sell directly to consumers on Amazon, either through FBM or FBA. DataHawk lets you connect an unlimited number of Seller Central accounts that you manage.

When you connect an account, DataHawk automatically collects and organizes:

- Financial data such as revenue, expenses, and product-level cost breakdowns
- Traffic and session data at product level
- FBA inventory levels and inventory movement data

## DataHawk connection capabilities

### Supported regions

DataHawk supports seller accounts in these Amazon regions:

- North America: US, Canada, Mexico, Brazil
- Europe: UK, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Netherlands, UAE, Sweden, India, Poland
- Far East: Japan, Singapore, Australia

### Data coverage

Metrics are exposed both as raw datasets and as ready-to-use dashboards and reports.

| Metric family | By account in your database | By SKU in your database |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sales | Yes | Yes |
| Orders | Yes | Yes |
| Units sold | Yes | Yes |
| Returns | Yes | Yes |
| Refunds and returns | Yes | Yes |
| Reimbursements | Yes | Yes |
| Marketing | Yes | Yes |
| Referral costs | Yes | Yes |
| Warehousing costs | Yes | Yes |
| Shipping costs | Yes | Yes |
| Other costs | Yes | Yes |
| Traffic and sessions | Yes | Yes |
| Inventory | Yes | Yes |

### Historical storage

When you connect a Seller Central account, DataHawk immediately retrieves up to 2 years of history for supported datasets, then stores and backs up data daily going forward.

### Refresh frequency and delay

- Refresh cadence: daily
- Last 24 to 48 hours can be incomplete

## DataHawk limitations

- Data for the last 48 hours may be incomplete
- Because of Amazon API constraints, some datasets can lag up to 72 hours
- Some metrics are limited by what Amazon exposes
- Initial history depth is limited by Amazon APIs

## Outputs

### Raw data

Connected Seller Central account data is available in these schemas:

| Schema | Available data |
| --- | --- |
| `FINANCE` | Sales data and profit and loss data |
| `RAW_INVENTORY` | Inventory breakdown data |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | Traffic and sessions data |

See the [data models overview](/data-models/overview) for the broader schema map.

### Dashboards

Ready-to-use dashboards are available in the web app and in the pre-built Power BI report covering seller finance and supply chain.
