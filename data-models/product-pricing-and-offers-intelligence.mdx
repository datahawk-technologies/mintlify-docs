## What this provides

This dataset helps you monitor product offers, prices, and featured offer information over time. You can track daily changes, understand market movement, and feed dashboards or automations.

### Key use cases

- Price monitoring and alerting
- Featured offer and Buy Box tracking
- Offer-level competition analysis
- Seller quality insights such as rating and feedback
- Long-term trend analysis from daily snapshots
- BI, reporting, and ML integrations

Reference: [Amazon featured offer overview](https://sell.amazon.com/blog/buy-box-featured-offer)

## Refresh frequency

Daily by default. Custom cadences such as twice daily or weekly can be configured on demand.

## History

History is retained moving forward from activation.

Pricing and offers intelligence is enabled by default for your tracked products.

> This data is available for any product on Amazon.
