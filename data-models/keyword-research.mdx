## Keyword research datasets

Use these datasets to improve product visibility and SEO strategy.

## Available datasets

Three core datasets are available for tracked products, plus one optional keyword-seeded dataset.

| Dataset | Description | Refresh frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Keyword suggestions | AI-generated keyword ideas with performance metrics | Daily |
| Keyword lookup | All keywords your products ranked for over the last 7 days | Weekly |
| Keyword placement | Where keywords appear in title, description, or bullets | Daily |
| Keyword-seeded keyword suggestions | Related keywords generated from a seed keyword | On demand |

Notes:

- Amazon stopped sharing placement data after August 16, 2023.
- Keyword-seeded keyword suggestions are available on request.

## Database tables

All data is in the `REPORTS` schema.

| Schema | Table | Contents |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `REPORTS` | `REPORT_PRODUCT_KEYWORD_SUGGESTIONS` | AI-generated keyword ideas |

## Key features

### Keyword suggestions

How DataHawk generates suggestions:

1. Identify product competitors.
2. Extract relevant keywords from competitor categories.
3. Filter out brand, color, and size-specific terms.
4. Score the remaining keywords for relevance.

Included metrics:

- Competitivity score from `-100` to `100`, based on the top three ranked products
- Monthly reach potential, combining search volume with competitivity

### Keyword lookup

Shows all keywords where your product:

- Ranked organically in the last 7 days
- Was detected by DataHawk tracking

### Keyword placement

Tracks whether keywords appear in:

- Title
- Description
- Bullet points
- Backend keywords

### Keyword-seeded keyword suggestions

This dataset generates related keywords from a seed keyword when you do not want to start from a product. It can replace part of the workflow teams often run in tools like SEMrush on top of DataHawk.

## Important notes

- Coverage: works for about 90% of products
- Supported marketplaces: English-speaking marketplaces only, including US, GB, CA, AU, SG, IN, and AE
- Processing time: track by noon UTC to get next-day results at 6am UTC

If suggestions are missing, DataHawk may need more data about the product's browse nodes or keyword rankings. In practice, that usually means refining categorization or tracking more relevant keywords.
