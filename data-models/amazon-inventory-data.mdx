This guide explains the Amazon inventory datasets available in DataHawk for tracking stock, reconciling discrepancies, and optimizing replenishment.

It includes:

1. A decision flowchart to map business questions to the right inventory tables.
2. Key inventory datasets, limitations, and use cases.
3. Table relationships between raw reports and transformed views.
4. Example SQL for common investigations.

Use this page to validate Amazon recommendations, audit ledger details, and reduce storage-fee risk.

> If you want to view your inventory at country level the same way Seller Central does, start with `SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_PLANNING` for FBA and `SELLER_INVENTORY_ALL_LISTINGS` for FBM.

## Inventory datasets

| Schema | View | Key columns | Purpose | Freshness | Geographic scope | Limitations | Example use case |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `RAW_INVENTORY` | `RAW_INVENTORY_FBA` | `asin`, `sku`, `afn_fulfillable_quantity`, `afn_inbound_shipped_quantity`, `afn_total_quantity` | Latest FBA snapshot for sellable, unsellable, and inbound units | Daily | Region | No country-level granularity, archived listings excluded, only ASINs seen in past 7 days | How much sellable stock do I have today |
| `RAW_INVENTORY` | `RAW_INVENTORY_FBA_HISTORY` | Same as above | Historical FBA snapshots | Daily | Region | Does not explain why changes occurred, archived listings excluded | How did my stock change over 30 days |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | `SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_HISTORY_DAILY` | `observation_date`, `asin`, `sku`, `afn_fulfillable_quantity`, `afn_inbound_shipped_quantity`, `afn_reserved_quantity`, `afn_total_quantity` | Daily seller-level FBA snapshots | Daily | Region | No transaction-level detail, no country or warehouse granularity | How did reserved inventory change over time |
| `RAW_INVENTORY` | `RAW_INVENTORY_RESTOCK_RECOMMENDATIONS` | `total_days_of_supply`, `recommended_replenishment_quantity`, `alert` | Amazon restock advice for SKUs with 14 days of supply or less | Daily | Country support in MX, FR, DE, CA, JP, US, IT, ES, GB | Excludes healthy SKUs and inactive SKUs | Which SKUs need urgent restocking |
| `RAW_INVENTORY` | `RAW_INVENTORY_RESTOCK_RECOMMENDATIONS_HISTORY` | `observation_time`, `recommended_replenishment_quantity`, `days_of_supply_at_amazon_fulfillment_network`, `total_days_of_supply` | History of Amazon restock advice | Daily | Same as above | Same as above | How has Amazon's restock advice changed |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | `SELLER_INVENTORY_LEDGER_DETAIL` | `event_type`, `quantity`, `fulfillment_center`, `disposition`, `country`, `adjustment_reason_code` | Detailed inventory movement ledger for the last 18 months | Daily, up to 3-day delay | Region, country, warehouse | None listed | Why is there a 200-unit discrepancy |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | `SELLER_INVENTORY_LEDGER_SUMMARY` | `starting_warehouse_balance`, `ending_warehouse_balance`, `customer_shipments`, `customer_returns` | Aggregated inventory health and net changes | Daily, up to 3-day delay | Region, country, warehouse | None listed | What is my net inventory change this week |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | `SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_PLANNING` | `inv_age_365_plus_days`, `recommended_action`, `estimated_storage_cost` | Recommendations for aged stock, pricing, and removals | Weekly | All except MX | MX not supported | Which SKUs should I remove to avoid fees |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | `SELLER_INVENTORY_ALL_LISTINGS` | `price`, `quantity`, `status` | Current catalog snapshot for MFN listings | Daily | Marketplace | No FBA quantity information | What products are active or inactive |
| `SELLING_PARTNER` | `SELLER_INVENTORY_ALL_LISTINGS_HISTORY` | `price`, `quantity`, `status` | Historical catalog snapshots for MFN listings | Daily | Marketplace | No FBA quantity information | What products were active last month |

## Example queries

### How much sellable AFN stock do I have for a SKU today

```sql
SELECT SKU, AFN_FULFILLABLE_QUANTITY, AFN_INBOUND_SHIPPED_QUANTITY
FROM RAW_INVENTORY_FBA
WHERE SKU = 'X';
```

### Why did my inventory drop by 200 units in the US last week

```sql
SELECT EVENT_TYPE, QUANTITY, FULFILLMENT_CENTER, DATE
FROM RAW_INVENTORY_LEDGER_DETAIL
WHERE SKU = 'X'
  AND COUNTRY = 'US'
  AND DATE BETWEEN '2023-10-01' AND '2023-10-07';
```

### Which SKUs need urgent restocking

```sql
SELECT SKU, ALERT, RECOMMENDED_REPLENISHMENT_QTY
FROM RAW_INVENTORY_RESTOCK_RECOMMENDATIONS
WHERE ALERT IN ('low_stock', 'out_of_stock');
```

If a SKU is missing here, check whether it has had sales in the last 3 months, has more than 14 days of supply, or has no projected demand.

### How has sellable stock changed over the past month

```sql
SELECT OBSERVATION_DATE, AFN_FULFILLABLE_QUANTITY
FROM SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_HISTORY_DAILY
WHERE ASIN = 'Y'
  AND OBSERVATION_DATE BETWEEN '2023-09-01' AND '2023-09-30';
```

### Which aged SKUs should I remove to avoid storage fees

```sql
SELECT SKU, INV_AGE_365_PLUS_DAYS, RECOMMENDED_ACTION, ESTIMATED_STORAGE_COST
FROM SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_PLANNING
WHERE INV_AGE_365_PLUS_DAYS > 0;
```

### Amazon says restock but I still have inventory

```sql
SELECT estimated_excess_quantity, weeks_of_cover_t30
FROM SELLING_PARTNER.SELLER_INVENTORY_FBA_PLANNING
WHERE SKU = 'YOUR_SKU';
```

Use this to reconcile restock recommendations against available stock and expected demand.
