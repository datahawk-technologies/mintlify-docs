## Overview

The Keyword Volumes dataset provides historical search volume metrics for keywords across Amazon marketplaces.

Use it to:

- Analyze keyword demand trends and seasonality
- Benchmark product visibility
- Inform advertising and SEO strategy
- Support product research and market-entry analysis

Each row represents the estimated monthly search volume for one keyword in one marketplace on one day.

Plural variants and misspellings are treated as separate keywords. For example, `bag` and `bags` are separate records.

## Requirements

- You must track the relevant keyword in DataHawk

## Supported marketplaces

- North America: all marketplaces
- Far East: all marketplaces
- Europe: Spain, United Kingdom, France, Netherlands, Germany, Italy, Sweden, Turkey, United Arab Emirates, India

## Data availability

- Most marketplaces: May 1, 2024
- AE and SG: August 15, 2024

## Data specification

Table:

- `SEO.SEO_KEYWORD_VOLUME_V2`

This dataset provides Amazon keyword search volume data. If a date or keyword has no data, its search volume is below measurable thresholds.

Granularity:

- One record per keyword, marketplace, and `DATE_DAY`

Frequency:

- Weekly refresh with the latest available search data

Dashboards:

- Search Power BI dashboard template
- Analytics Essentials Looker Studio dashboard
