---
title: "Vendor Account - Sourcing vs Manufacturing View"
---

This page explains the difference between sourcing and manufacturing in Amazon Vendor Central and why it affects what DataHawk can display.

## Sourcing

Sourcing covers products Amazon buys directly from your vendor account, so the view reflects only your contribution to sales.

## Manufacturing

Manufacturing covers the total product performance on Amazon for products tied to your brand, regardless of who supplied Amazon.
