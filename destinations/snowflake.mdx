## Snowflake overview

Snowflake is a fully managed, cloud-native data warehouse built for performance and scale. It gives you flexible analytics with very little operational overhead.

## Your DataHawk Snowflake database

DataHawk provides a fully managed Snowflake database with:

- Daily updated tables
- Optimized views prepared for analytics
- No infrastructure management on your side

## Access your Snowflake credentials

In your DataHawk workspace, go to `Setup > Destinations > Snowflake > See Credentials`.

## Supported integrations

Snowflake connects well with modern analytics tooling. Typical examples include:

### Business intelligence

- Power BI
- Metabase
- Sigma Computing

### SQL and analytics platforms

- Hex
- Deepnote
- Streamlit

### Data science and ML

- dbt
- Python notebooks
- R notebooks

Your warehouse is ready to use for dashboards, reports, and custom analytics as soon as credentials are available.

## Alternative setup: self-hosted Snowflake

You can also connect your own Snowflake account to DataHawk. DataHawk will then load the same collected and enriched datasets into your Snowflake environment on a daily cadence.

To start, contact your account manager and share:

- Your Snowflake [account identifier](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/admin-account-identifier)
- Your Snowflake hosting provider or cloud environment, such as Azure
