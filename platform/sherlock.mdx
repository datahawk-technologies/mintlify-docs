Sherlock is DataHawk's AI-powered diagnostic engine. It detects meaningful product-level performance changes, explains likely root causes, and recommends what to do next.

## Main goals

- Spot major revenue increases and drops
- Explain the drivers across traffic, conversion, pricing, ads, Buy Box, and inventory
- Prioritize action by impact rather than by raw volume of alerts

## Core themes

- Conversion fading
- Rank decay
- Buy Box instability
- Ad-spend friction
- Inventory misalignment
