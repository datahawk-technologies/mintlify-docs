---
title: "Alerts"
description: "Create daily email notifications for important product changes."
---

Alerts monitor tracked products on Amazon and Walmart and send daily notifications when selected changes occur.

## Common alert categories

- Buy Box and seller changes
- Pricing changes
- Listing detail changes
- Ratings and reviews
- Stock and availability changes

## Custom triggers

Build rules from a property, an operator, and a threshold or target value.
