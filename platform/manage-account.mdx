---
title: "Manage your Account"
description: "Update your personal account details from the Settings area."
---

Use **Settings** > **Account** to manage your DataHawk user profile.

## Available actions

- Update your name
- Update your email
- Change your password
- Delete your account
- Download your personal data export

## Password rules

Your password must include at least six characters, one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.
