---
title: "Workspace Management"
description: "Create and manage isolated workspaces for brands, clients, or business units."
---

Workspaces keep data, settings, and access separated for each brand, client, or operating segment.

## Common tasks

- Create a new workspace from the workspace picker or from **Settings** > **Workspace**
- Review workspace capabilities and current usage
- Request an upgrade when you reach your contracted limits

## Important note

New workspaces start fresh. They do not inherit settings from existing workspaces.
