title: "Insights"
description: "Receive proactive findings, anomalies, and good-practice guidance."
---

Insights are proactive notifications based on automated data exploration across ads, products, sales, SEO, and market data.

## Insight types

- **Findings** for business-savvy recommendations tied to your data
- **Anomalies** for unexpected changes and outliers
- **Good Practices** for curated eCommerce guidance that is not account-specific

## Collaboration features

You can filter insights, assign them to teammates, and track status through states such as `New`, `In Progress`, `Done`, `Expected`, and `No Action Needed`.

## Delivery

DataHawk sends a daily email summary of newly detected insights on business days.
